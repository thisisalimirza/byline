Features

Online-format writing rules

Modeled on Dickie Bush and Nicolas Cole's framework: short paragraphs, mandatory subheadings, hooks that land in 2 sentences. Enforced at the writer and humanizer stage.

Keyword placement in the first 100 words, in subheadings, and throughout the body. The final agent scores your title and meta description and flags anything off-target.

FAQ

Questions & answers

Do I need an Anthropic account?

Yes — you'll need an Anthropic API key from console.anthropic.com. You enter it in Byline and it's used directly. Byline never stores or logs your key.

How much does it cost per article?

That depends on which Claude model you select. A full pipeline run with Sonnet typically uses around 12,000–18,000 tokens total across all five agents — a few cents per article at API pricing.

Will the articles actually rank on Google?

Byline produces articles optimized for SEO — but ranking depends on your domain authority, competition, and how well you publish and promote the content. Byline handles the on-page writing; you handle the distribution.

Can I edit the AI prompts?

Yes. Every agent's system prompt is fully editable via the Advanced Settings section. You can customize the strategist's brief format, the writer's rules, or the SEO editor's output structure.

How does the product promotion work?

You enter your product name, description, and URL. Choosing a promotion style (Soft, Contextual, or Convert) injects style-specific instructions into the strategist, writer, and humanizer — so the mention feels organic, not inserted.

Which CMS platforms are supported?

The article preview includes step-by-step publishing guides for WordPress, Webflow, Ghost, Substack, and a generic guide for any CMS. You can also copy the article as Markdown, HTML, or plain text.

Is there a word count limit?

The writer agent is prompted to produce 800–1,100 words by default. You can edit the system prompt to request longer or shorter articles — the humanizer and SEO editor will adapt accordingly.

How is Byline different from ChatGPT or other AI writers?

Most AI writers produce one pass of text. Byline runs five specialized agents sequentially — strategy, research, writing, humanizing, and SEO editing — each with strict rules that the previous stage can't override.
